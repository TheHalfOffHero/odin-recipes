Baked Pineapple

Description

Pineapple is a fantastic fruit. Even if you dislike it on pizza eating it on its own can be an absolute delite. This baked pineapple recipe proves that pineapple if prepared properly is a killer side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 pound butter, softened
  • 4 eggs, beaten
  • 1 (20 ounce) can pineapple
  • 5 slices bread, crusts removed and broken into crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. In a medium bowl cream sugar and butter. Mix in eggs. Drain and add pineapple, then mix in the bread crumbs. Transfer mixture to a 10-inch pie plate or baking dish.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 45 to 60 minutes, or until browned.
